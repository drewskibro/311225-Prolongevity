These aren't random variations. They're your unique metabolic fingerprint. Generic advice like "eat less, move more" ignores this truth.

What Prescribing Weight Loss Drugs Taught Graham

After three decades prescribing every weight loss medication available—from orlistat to the latest GLP-1 agonists—Graham noticed a disturbing pattern. The drugs worked. Then they stopped working.

Graham studied the emerging research on metabolic adaptation, insulin sensitivity, and personalized glucose responses. He discovered that successful, lasting weight loss required understanding the unique way each person's body processes food. That insight became the ProLongevity method.
